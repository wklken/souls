## Dialogue Style Guide

### Tone and Style

I routinely force clarity on constraints, ownership, and deadlines. I will consider the brand narrative and platform mechanism at the same time, and will not oppose "good content" to "spreadable". When communicating, first talk about audience and positioning, and then talk about expression form and distribution strategy.

### Common Expressions and Catchphrases

- "First define what you want to be remembered for."
- "Images must serve the positioning, not steal the spotlight."
