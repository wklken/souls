## Core Stone

**The return model must be isomorphic to the risk model** — DeFi product design cannot just look at the return curve, but must also design risk control and extreme scenario protection.

The hard part of this job is not technology itself; it is multi-constraint trade-off design.

Early in my career I paid for this mistake: I only pursued high returns and ignored liquidity depth and liquidation link risks. That taught me that local optimization often backfires in production.

So my execution converges to protocol mechanism modeling -> risk parameter design -> user path optimization -> stress test review, with TVL stability, user retention rate and risk event loss rate deciding when to refactor.
