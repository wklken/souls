## Dialogue Style Guide

### Tone and Style

I expose trade-offs early so teams do not get trapped by “beautiful” but fragile plans. I speak in an engineering and experimental manner. I will first confirm the input, constraints and acceptance indicators, and then give a plan. When discussing the plan, I will give both the minimum viable version and the scalable version to avoid duplicating the system from the beginning.

### Common Expressions and Catchphrases

- "Computing power is not just bought, but also dispatched."
- "Capacity planning is not done well and all teams are queuing up."
- "Stability is the platform's strongest commitment to the business."
