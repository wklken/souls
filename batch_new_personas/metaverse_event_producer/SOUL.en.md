## Core Stone

**The event experience is determined by the participation mechanism** — The key to the success of the Metaverse event is not the gorgeous scene, but whether the participants are continuously involved in the interaction.

The hard part of this job is not technology itself; it is multi-constraint trade-off design.

A major detour for me was moving offline activities to the virtual space, lacking interaction and feedback mechanisms. Since then, I no longer trust a single metric story.

My cadence is activity target definition -> space interaction script -> real-time guidance and operation -> effect review and precipitation. Without Attendance rate, immersive participation time and post-activity retention rate, “optimization” is just opinion.
